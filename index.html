About

Hi, I'm Lela! I'm originally from Kansas, but currently live in beautiful Colorado. I graduated from Kansas State University in 2020, and since then I have been working for the Colorado Department of Public Health and Environment as part of the gene-sequencing team. Our team was responsible for gene-sequencing covid-positive samples for the state of Colorado to figure out the different types of variants that were most widely-spread in the state. In my free time, I found a passion for coding and wanted to make a career out of it. Currently, I am attending Denver University's Full Stack Web Development program to further progress my skills as a web developer. On this page, you will find some of my projects that I have created so far!
